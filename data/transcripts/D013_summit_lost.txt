CALL TRANSCRIPT - Summit Health Partners Deal Lost Call
Date: November 20, 2025
Attendees: Sarah Chen (AE), Mike Anderson (VP Sales, Summit Health Partners)
Duration: 18 minutes

Mike Anderson: Sarah, I hate making this call. I've been trying to get this across the line for six months and I just couldn't get it done.

Sarah Chen: I appreciate you telling me directly, Mike. What happened?

Mike Anderson: The CFO killed it. We needed her sign-off on anything over $100K and she just wouldn't engage. I got three meetings on her calendar and she cancelled every single one. My CRO tried to escalate it and even he couldn't get the meeting.

Sarah Chen: Was it a budget issue specifically or a priority issue?

Mike Anderson: Both. She's dealing with a revenue miss this year and has frozen all new vendor spending over $100K. She told my CRO that anything in the revenue stack needs to wait until next fiscal year which starts in April.

Sarah Chen: Did the conversation with Gong come up as an alternative?

Mike Anderson: Yeah, that was the twist. After the CFO killed our deal, one of our directors found Gong and pitched it as a smaller engagement — just conversation intelligence at $55K which was under the $100K approval threshold. The CFO approved that because it didn't need her sign-off. So we're getting Gong for CI and we'll look at the full platform conversation with you in April.

Sarah Chen: So the total platform wasn't the objection — it was the price point hitting a threshold that required an executive who wouldn't engage?

Mike Anderson: Exactly. If your product had a $50K entry point for conversation intelligence only, I could have gotten it through without the CFO. But at $180K for the full platform, it needed her approval and she wasn't going to give it to anyone this year.

Sarah Chen: That's really helpful feedback. Would you be open to reconnecting in February to plan for the April budget cycle?

Mike Anderson: Absolutely. I still want the full platform. Set something up for early February and I'll make sure the CRO is on the call too. I just need to get the budget locked in for next fiscal year.

Sarah Chen: I'll do that. And Mike — thanks for fighting for this internally. That kind of champion advocacy matters.

Mike Anderson: Thanks Sarah. We'll get this done next year.
